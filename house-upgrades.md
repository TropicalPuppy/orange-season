# House Upgrades

Raphael can upgrade your house up to 2 times. You can buy the upgrades by going to his shop after 12:30AM.

## Kitchen Upgrade

***Materials and Cost:***

-   x200 Branch
-   x50 Brick
-   x30 Roof Tile
-   $6000
